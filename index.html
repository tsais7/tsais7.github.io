In The Beginning

Org Mode

I've been using GNU Emacs for quite some time now, but never touched Org Mode, just didn't have a compelling reason to do so. But I've always wanted a blog, and what better ways to do it other than using Org?

Org is incredible, and I've barely scratched the surface. It doesn't get in my way, and Emacs being self documenting C-h m makes it easy to ease into org. I quickly noticed how so many modern notetaking/productivity apps that tries so hard just to mimic a fraction of Emacs/Org's power. Pathetic and bloat. Electron was a mistake.
